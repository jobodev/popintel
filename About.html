About PopIntel Automations

Population Health Analytics • Automation • Incentive Optimization

Our Mission

PopIntel Automations was created to solve one of the biggest challenges in modern Population Health: **FQHCs are drowning in manual reporting, fragmented systems, and underutilized incentive revenue.**

Our mission is simple — build automated, scalable analytics systems that give clinical and administrative teams instant visibility into performance, care gaps, UDS metrics, HEDIS measures, and high-impact incentive opportunities.

What We Do

We develop Python pipelines, SQL frameworks, and Power BI dashboards that automate the entire Population Health workflow — from raw Athena/Snowflake data to clean actionable lists used by care coordinators, providers, and administrative leadership.

Automated UDS & HEDIS Pipelines

Real-time care gap lists, clean patient rollups, encounter logic, and monthly reconciliation.

Payor Incentive Optimization

Forecasts for Florida Blue, Medicaid, Medicare, Sunshine, Humana, UHC, and more.

Provider Performance Dashboards

Scorecards, clinic views, high-risk patients, missed opportunities, and trend visualization.

Workflow & Automation Design

Population Health operational system design for sustainable, scalable success.
